Fortune System

  • Fading Symbols

    Hit the right combos and the junk symbols just fade away. This creates visual space and a feeling of gradual clarity. A Removal Indicator shows what has already faded, helping you sense how the field is opening up.

  • Rising Energy Path

    Each successful moment lights a segment along a calm, visual path. Once enough segments glow, the multiplier softly increases. If the rhythm breaks, the light fades — not abruptly, but naturally — returning the system to balance.

  • Dream Continuation

    During special dream-like phases, the multiplier continues to grow as long as movement remains uninterrupted. When the flow ends, the energy settles, and the valley returns to stillness.

The Philosophy Of Luck

The game wants you to find your groove instead of obsessing over winning. Those who stay calm tend to go further.
